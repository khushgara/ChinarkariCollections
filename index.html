About Our Kashmiri Clothing Collection

At Chinarkari, we bridge the gap between age-old Kashmiri traditions and modern sartorial needs. Each piece in our collection is a tribute to the valley's rich textile heritage, meticulously handcrafted by master artisans whose skills have been passed down through generations.

Our embroidery techniques ranging from the delicate Sozni (needle-work) to the majestic Tilla (metallic thread-work) define the premium nature of our couture. We utilize only the finest base materials including authentic Pashmina, softest Silks, and regal Velvets.
